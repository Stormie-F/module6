My Research

I am passionate about making academic topics accessible to everyone. I am especially interested in the intersection of queer history and traditional genealogical research. I did this video as part of a final project for Social Issues in Archives. I have been working on other video essays about other queer folks in history, both well-known and those hidden in the records....

Learn More

Some of my favorite resources for research include:

  • The Digital Transgender Archive --The DTA is a wonderful collection of queer history from archives in multiple countries.
  • A Rainbow Colored Thread --This source book collects queer Jewish history from the earliest written records to the modern era.
  • Fordam's "People with a History source page --A digital collection of queer source texts from spanning global history.
